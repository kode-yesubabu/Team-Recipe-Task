### Quick Vegetable Biryani Recipe

**Ingredients**

* 1 cup basmati rice
* 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
* 1 large onion, sliced
* 1 tomato, chopped
* 2 tbsp yogurt
* 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
* 1 tsp biryani masala
* ½ tsp turmeric
* ½ tsp red chili powder
* 2 tbsp oil or ghee
* Whole spices: 1 bay leaf, 2 cloves, 1 small cinnamon stick
* Salt to taste
* Fresh coriander and mint leaves

### Steps

1. Wash and soak the rice for 20 minutes.
2. Heat oil/ghee and sauté the whole spices.
3. Add onions and cook until golden.
4. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato, turmeric, chili powder, biryani masala, and yogurt.
5. Add vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
6. Add soaked rice, salt, and about 1¾ cups water.
7. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is tender and the water is absorbed.
8. Garnish with coriander and mint leaves before serving.
